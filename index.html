About PharmaSymbiosis

The founding members of PharmaSymbiosis bring with them a cumulative experience of more than 70 years of experience working with multinational pharmaceutical and diagnostics companies. The formation of the company was inspired by the increasing opportunities in the healthcare sector in Africa, as evidenced by:

  • The rapidly increasing population and disease burden
  • The major need for improved Quality Assurance in the healthcare services value chain
  • The increasing focus and commitment to doing business in a compliant way
  • The increasing political commitment to improve access to healthcare
  • The increasing middle-class population

At PharmaSymbiosis

  • We have the experience of working as Marketing Representatives, Country Managers, Public Health Managers, Policymakers, Regulators and Academics.
  • We have knowledge and understanding of the Healthcare market in Africa
  • We have access to major stakeholders and decision makers.
  • We understand what you need to deliver a compliant and sustainable business

OUR COVERAGE

  • Pharmasymbiosis Limited is fully set up in Ghana, Nigeria and Ivory Coast with plans to set up in Kenya and Sierra Leone by the end of the year 2020. We have an ambition to spread to other sub-Sahara Africa Countries
